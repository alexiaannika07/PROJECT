Why This Advocacy?

This advocacy is all about caring for our planet and showing love for our Earth. Earth is of utmost importance, and Earthly is here to prove that. Channel your voices to us, and your efforts and we promise it will not be wasted.

You can help by actively volunteering in our clean up drives, stationed in different parts and areas of the country. If you can’t actively volunteer but want to help our cause, you can donate funds to help our active volunteers. Proceeds will surely be used for this advocacy and this advocacy only.

But, we call to you and your families that in your own little ways, please help clean and lessen the pollution in our environment. Let’s learn to segregate our trash at our own homes, avoid burning leaves and paper, learn to pick up trash, and all others.

We should care for our Mother Earth and this is where we can start. Join us, be an agent of Earth!
